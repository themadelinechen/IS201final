my travels

wednesday, june 19, 2024 6:48pm

recently, i got the opportunity to visit new york city for a week and it was a life changing experience.

i mainly went because of a networking trip through the byu finance program. it was so interesting seeing all the different different investment banks and private equity firms. that can be me one day, working in the financial district of the city.

but the best part of the trip were the relationships that i formed and revisited there. i met many interesting people and got to visit the friends and people that i love.

i love nyc, but most importantly i love the people of nyc.
